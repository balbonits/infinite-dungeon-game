The town lore was established in April 2026: frontier settlement, newly founded as an "adventurers' town" beside a recently discovered natural cave that leads into an infinite dungeon. Player is the first resident adventurer/delver. NPCs are pioneers who chose to come here, not generic established merchants.

**Why:** Gives the world a sense of purpose and stakes. The player isn't just another adventurer in a city -- they're THE explorer this entire settlement was built to support. NPCs feel like real people with motivations.

**How to apply:** All NPC dialogue, world-building text, and future lore should reflect the frontier/pioneer tone. The dungeon is uncharted and mysterious. The town is new and raw. Future town expansion feature (post-MVP) should tie to player depth milestones.
